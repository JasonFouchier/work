Jason's Favourite Recipe

Spaghetti and meat sauce

Spaghetti and meat sauce or meatballs has been my favourite meal for as long as I can remember, It is also one of the few meals that I can actually cook. :)

I have always felt that nothing says "home cooked meal" like a great plate of spaghetti and meat sauce

Here is a list of ingredients that you'll need to prepare this quick meal

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Pasta sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Montreal steak spice

Now to put it all together, here are the directions for cooking

  1. Brown your ground beef in a fry pan
  2. Put the desired amount of pasta into a pot 3/4 full of water, add a pinch of salt and a cap full of olive oil. Brng the water to a boil stiring occasionally until noodles are soft.
  3. Add your spices (salt, pepper, Montreal Steak spice) to your cooking beef, to your desired taste
  4. Once your ground beef is cooked, stir in one jar of your favourite pasta sauce
  5. Add in 1/2 a cup or more of the Parmesan cheese (depending how cheesy you want your sauce)
  6. Let your sauce simmer, stiring occasionally
  7. Drain the water from your pot of pasta nodles
  8. Put your desired amount of pasta on a plate, top with your cheesy meat sauce and enjoy!

Please feel free to change or experiment with this recipe, make it your own
